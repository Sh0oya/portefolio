# AI that saves your team time.

> No IT department, no big budget, no jargon. We start small on one process, from 400 EUR, with EU hosting and one human contact. Useful AI for a small business, not an IT overhaul.

## Concrete use-cases: three ways to use AI in a small business

Not abstract AI: assistants that handle one specific task, every day, and give hours back to your team. We pick together the one that saves you the most time first.

### Customer service: answer instantly

An assistant that answers your customers' common questions right away, from your own information: hours, lead times, pricing, order tracking, FAQ. Your team only handles the cases that truly matter.

- Instant answers, day and night
- Grounded in your own information
- Hands over to a human when needed
- On your site, email or chat

Saves: the hours spent answering the same basic questions over and over.

### Sales: qualify and follow up (highest return)

An assistant that sorts and qualifies your inbound requests, drafts the reply and follows up with prospects at the right time. Your salespeople spend their time selling, not sorting.

- Automatic lead qualification
- First message and follow-ups drafted
- Prioritises the hottest requests
- Plugs into your CRM or inbox

Saves: the leads lost to missed follow-ups and the time spent sorting requests.

### Internal ops: your documents, on demand

An assistant that answers your team's questions from your own documents and procedures: manuals, standard contracts, processes, history. No more hunting for the right file for ten minutes.

- Answers from your internal documents
- Cites the source of every answer
- Available to the whole team, simply
- Data siloed, access controlled

Saves: the time lost finding information, and the onboarding of new joiners.

We don't do all three at once. We pick the process that saves you the most time, do it well, then extend if it's worth it.
