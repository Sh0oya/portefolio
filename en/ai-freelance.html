FAQ

Questions before starting.

Why a solo AI freelance rather than a team?

You talk directly to the person who designs and writes the code, no project manager, no subcontracting. Decisions take one conversation. For a very large product run by several teams in parallel, an agency remains the better fit, and I will say so.

How fast is it delivered?

A simple AI agent or a focused automation: one week. A full application with built-in AI: 4 to 6 weeks, quoted per project, with a usable first version in production then iterations after real-world use. The exact timeline is set at scoping.

Do you work remotely and in English?

Yes. Scoping, demos and documentation happen over video, in French or English, with code and documentation available online.

Do you work with startups as well as SMEs?

Yes. The format adapts: a focused automation or AI agent for an SME, a full MVP for a startup. In both cases, a first version reaches production quickly, then evolves through iterations.

What if my need does not justify custom development?

I tell you at scoping. Sometimes an existing tool, properly configured, is enough; in that case I point you to it. Sometimes the right answer is to build nothing.

Talk about your AI project?

Describe your need in 30 seconds. I will tell you if I can do it, how long it takes and in which format, with no commitment.
