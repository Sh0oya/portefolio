FAQ

Questions I get often.

What if I don’t know exactly what I need?

That is the most common situation. You do not need a written spec: just describe the outcome you are aiming for, in plain business terms. The 30-minute scoping call turns a fuzzy idea into a clear perimeter, and you leave with a clear recommendation, with no commitment. If the project is not ready, I say so and point you in the right direction.

Why not an agency?

An agency bills 3 to 5 times more for the same deliverable, because it has to fund salespeople, project managers and back-office. Here, you have a single point of contact: the person who scopes, codes and delivers. Decisions move faster. For a product that needs more than ten developers in parallel, an agency remains the right choice; for an MVP, an internal tool or an automation, it is not.

How do you guarantee code quality?

I rely on modern tools to write and document faster. I review, test and validate every delivery before it goes to production. You receive readable, documented code.

What tools do you work with?

Proven building blocks: Next.js, Supabase and Stripe for applications, n8n for automations, AI agents on the OpenAI or Claude APIs, and Claude Code as a development assistant.

Who owns the code, and where is the data hosted?

The code is yours: a Git repository in your name, admin access, clear documentation. Your data can be hosted in France, with GDPR compliance built in from the start. Everything is transferable to another team, at any time.

What if I already have a tech team?

Then I step in as targeted reinforcement (AI integration, CRM rebuild, automation), alongside your team. Collaboration runs through a shared workspace and a short weekly check-in.

Do we need to sign a contract before starting?

Yes. Every engagement starts with a written quote and, if you want, a signed non-disclosure agreement before any detailed discussion about your product. Nothing begins on a verbal handshake alone.

Do you work remotely and in English?

Yes. I am based in Paris and work remotely with clients across France and internationally. Exchanges happen over video, and English calls are fine from scoping through to demos.

Talk now?

Describe your project in 30 seconds. I will tell you whether I can do it, and how long it takes, within 24 hours.
