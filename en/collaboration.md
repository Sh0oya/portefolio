## FAQ: questions I get often

### Why not an agency?

An agency bills 3 to 5x more for the same deliverable, because it has to fund salespeople, project managers, designers and back-office. For an MVP or an internal tool, it's the wrong vehicle. For a product needing 10+ devs in parallel, sure, hire an agency.

### Why not a regular Malt freelancer?

Many classical freelancers are single-stack and single-role. I handle scoping, simple design, frontend and backend code, infra, and AI integration. A whole project becomes manageable by one person.

### What about code quality, since you vibe-code?

Vibe coding doesn't mean "Claude writes everything, I click go". It's pair-programming: I guide, review, test, refactor. Claude Code accelerates typing and docs, not judgment. Everything goes through me before prod.

### Who owns the code?

Your code is your code. Git repo you own, admin access, clear docs. If you want to hand it over tomorrow, everything is transferable with no hidden dependencies.

### What if I already have a tech team?

Perfect, I can plug in as an additional builder on a focused topic (AI integration, CRM rebuild, automation). Many of my current missions are alongside existing teams.

### Do you work on English projects?

Yes. My English is professional, I work regularly with English-speaking stacks (Claude API, GitHub, Stripe). English calls are fine for scoping and demos, I can write docs in EN if needed.

### Can you sign an NDA?

Yes, no problem. I sign before any detailed discussion about your product or data. Just ask at the start.
