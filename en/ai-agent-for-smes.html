FAQ

The questions an SME asks.

Isn't AI too complex for my small business?

No. You need neither an IT department nor in-house technical skills. You describe the need in plain business terms, I handle the technical side, and you get a tool that is simple to use day to day. We start with a single process so adoption stays straightforward.

Isn't it too expensive for a small company?

A first agent on one focused process starts from 400 EUR with the one-week Sprint format. We measure the time saved, then extend only if the results justify it.

What happens to my data and my customers' data?

It stays yours. The agent only accesses the data it strictly needs, what is processed is documented, and nothing is resold or reused for other purposes. You keep full control.

Will an AI agent replace my staff?

No. The agent takes on the repetitive tasks: answering common questions, sorting, qualifying, drafting a first version. Your team keeps what needs human judgment: relationships, complex cases, decisions.

How long before I see results?

On a well-chosen first process, a useful agent ships in about a week. The time saved shows from the first days of real use, and we adjust based on field feedback.

Who owns the agent once it's delivered?

Yours. Everything is transferable if you want to hand maintenance to someone else tomorrow. Evolutions can run through a monthly support plan, at your own pace.

Got a process in mind? Let's talk.

Describe the task that takes the most of your team's time. I'll reply with an opinion and a quote, with no commitment.
